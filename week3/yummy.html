Where to find the best Vietnamese Coffee in SoCal!

Yummy Bakery


Besides Yummy Bakery's very yummy (ahha...) coffee, many people also enjoy their food options, like the Vietnamese sandwiches (Banh Mi) and steamed pork buns (Banh Bao). While they do have a more narrow drink selection, they make up for it in the quality of coffee AND food. Yummy Bakery's signature House coffee does not disappoint. It's as if their coffee is made directly in Vietnam! Most of the locations are also centered around Little Saigon due to the large population of Vietnamese Americans, but new locations are rapidly popping up in the SoCal area!


Here are some other popular/signature drinks AND dishes served at Yummy Bakery:


  •  Banh Bao w/ Pork and Salted Duck Egg
  •  Combination Bao
  •  Banh Mi Yummy
  •  Vietnamese Three Color Dessert (Che Ba Mau)
  •  Thai tea
