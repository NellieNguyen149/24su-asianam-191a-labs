Lab 2: My favorite cafes in Orange County!

POV Nellie tries to convince you to try her faves...


  • Phin Smith: A well-known local business specializing in Phin drip Vietnamese coffee, Matcha lattes, and many other classics.
  • 7 Leaves: Founded by a family of first-generation immigrants, they started from inspirational, humble beginnings to serve iconic drinks including: Vietnamese coffee, sea salt Jasmine tea, and green thai tea.
  • Brewstory: A recent cafe that opened up near the beach! I heard they import matcha directly from Japan :0
